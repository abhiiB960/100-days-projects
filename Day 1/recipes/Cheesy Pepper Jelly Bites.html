Cheesy Pepper Jelly Bites

Cheesy Pepper Jelly Bites are delicious little appetizers that combine creamy, melted cheese with the sweet and spicy kick of pepper jelly, all nestled in a flaky pastry shell. The contrast between the buttery crust, gooey cheese, and tangy jelly makes every bite irresistible and perfectly balanced in flavor and texture.

History

These bite-sized treats are quick to prepare and ideal for parties, game nights, or festive gatherings. Whether made with puff pastry or phyllo cups, they look elegant yet require minimal effort. Ingredients

  • Pepper jelly - adds a sweet and spicy flavor on top.
  • Puff pastry or phyllo cups - the crispy, flaky base.
  • Cream cheese or brie - for the rich, creamy filling.

Steps for preparation

  1. Prepare the base - Cut and place puff pastry or phyllo cups on a baking tray.
  2. Add filling - Spoon a bit of cream cheese or brie into each cup, then top with pepper jelly.
  3. Bake - Cook in the oven until the pastry is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
